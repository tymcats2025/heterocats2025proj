Welcome to the World of Heterochromia Cats!

This is a fun and educational page dedicated to showcasing the beauty and uniqueness of cats with heterochromia! Heterochromia is a rare condition where a cat has two different colored eyes or multiple colors within the same eye.

Through this page, you can learn more about how heterochromia occurs, view some adorable images of cats with this unique feature, and even discover more about the science behind eye color in animals!

Why Heterochromia?

Many people are fascinated by the stunning and unusual eye colors of these cats. Heterochromia can make them appear magical or otherworldly. It's caused by differences in melanin production, which affects the pigmentation of their eyes. The condition is most commonly seen in cats with breeds like Turkish Van, Odd-eyed White, and more.
