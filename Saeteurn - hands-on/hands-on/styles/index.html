About This Page

Welcome to this fun and informative page all about heterochromia in cats! Heterochromia is a condition where a cat has two different colored eyes, and sometimes one eye is multicolored. It's a rare and stunning condition, making these cats truly unique!

Box Model Example

Heterochromia occurs when there is an uneven distribution of melanin in the eyes, leading to one eye being a different color than the other. Some cats even have multiple colors in one eye, making them all the more fascinating!
